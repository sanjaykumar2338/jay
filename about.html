About Us

Headquartered in Nashville, TN, Isurison is a leading-edge college planning technology company. Our goal is to revolutionize the industry by providing best-in-class solutions that utilize proprietary analytics for today's college planning problems. By harnessing a team of college counselors, coupled with highly skilled computer scientists, we are able to deliver breakthrough innovations with the right expertise to solve specific needs.

Our team of college counselors have helped students get accepted to schools such as Harvard University, University of Pennsylvania, Carnegie Mellon University, and Yale University to name a few and we want to help you succeed too. With each solution that we develop, we strive to exceed your expectations and welcome your feedback in this endeavor. Please contact us today if you have any questions or comments.
